Hello everyone! Recently I've been trying to get back into collecting Pokemon cards since
it's been around 4-5 years when I last was collecting them consistently. The last time I was
collecting Pokemon cards, it was the X and Y generation of Pokemon so it's been a little since
the generation now is Sword and Shield. But before I start expanding my collection again, I
decided to take a look back at the cards I have just to see where my personal collection
stands as of right now.

Over the past couple of days I've been looking at my collection very closely and researching all
of the rare cards I have. I have all of my regular basic cards in a white binder, each in their
own card sleeve, along with most of my reverse holographic cards. My regular holographic cards and
other ultra rare cards like full arts, level X's, and EX's are in their own seperate card holder that's
Pokemon themed(If you're unsure on what exactly I mean by those cards, I will provide pictured examples
below this post). I didn't think that the cards I have would be worth much but boy was I wrong. One of
my rare cards is a Charizard Lv. X and also a Blaziken Lv. X that I traded for in 6th grade and when
looking up the prices for the Charizard card my jaw dropped when I saw people selling this card I have
for up to $100+! Since mine isn't in mint condition it definitely won't be worth as much but it definitely
is at least worth around $75. The other cards I have do have some good prices too ranging from $5 to $30
but the two mentioned above are the most important in my collection. Looking to the future, hopefully I
can get some more cards really soon and pull some really cool and rare cards to increase the value of my
collection and to expand it. Wish me luck in the opening of my card packs in the near future!
