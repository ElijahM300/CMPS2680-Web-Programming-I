Hello all you gamers! In this post I wanted to talk about some of the games I've been playing recently
and what my favorite game of all time is and why. So to start, the main games I've been playing recently
are Dead By Daylight and Overwatch on my PS4 and Animal Crossing: New Horizons on my Switch. I don't play
Overwatch as often as the other two right now but once the Winter Wonderland event for this year comes out
I'll definitely be playing it a lot more than I am right now. My main focus right now is Dead By Daylight
because I'm trying to finish what's called the Rift in this game which is just essentially a battle pass
like what Fortnite has. The good thing though is that there's only 70 tiers and I'm on tier 39 of the Rift
with 25 days left to go so I should be able to complete it. For those of you that don't know what Dead By
Daylight is, it's an asymmetrical horror game where it's one killer against four survivors and the goal for
the killer is to, well, kill everyone and for the survivors, you need to repair five generators that power
up exit gates so that you can escape. I think the game is very fun and cool since you have classic licensed
killers in this game like Michael Myers or Ghostface and even the Demogorgon from Stranger Things is a killer
in this game. Moving on to Animal Crossing, winter is now on people's islands and with it comes a lot of new
DIY recipes for you to make so you can decorate your island with plenty of Christmas stuff. Each day you can
make a snowman and if you make them right, they'll give you a DIY recipe to craft and a large snowflake which
is essential for the recipes that snowmen can give you. What's known as Toy Day is on Christmas Eve and you have
to basically be Santa and deliver toys and gifts to all of your villagers and in return you'll get a special
item.

So, now that what I'm playing now is out of the way, I wanted to talk about what my favorite game of all
time is and why it's my favorite. I've played a numerous amount of games from when I started to play video games
so picking my favorite was a tough choice but for me, I would have to go with Paper Mario: The Thousand Year Door.
Simply put, I think this game is one of the best games ever made because for a Mario game, the plot is surprisingly
dark and very interesting which is a nice change from what you would usually be used to in a Mario game. Also, the
game is an action/adventure rpg so turn-based battling is the main focus for combat. The world, the characters, and
how unique and original this game is makes this game a very good time all around and I would highly, HIGHLY, recommend
this game to anyone who hasn't played it before and trust me, you won't be disappointed with it. Well, that's all for
this post so thank you for reading this and before I end this post, below you will find trivia for basic video game
knowledge with five questions so have fun with that and good luck!
