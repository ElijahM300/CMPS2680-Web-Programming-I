Hello everyone! Today I wanted to go through some of my favorite anime's that I've watched so far and to tell you
guys which one is my most favorite out of all of them. To start, the very first anime that I ever really watched
was Kaguya Sama: Love is War. The second season came out a while ago and while I still haven't watched it yet, I have
watched all of season 1 so let's get into it. Love is War is a really good RomCom anime that never falls short of being
able to deliver humor and the characters in this show are also very funny and interesting to watch interact with one
another. Overall, this anime has got humor and heart which is why it's one of my favorites. Now, on to the next anime,
I would have to say that another favorite of mine is Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid. This anime also has a lot of heart
and humor and is just an overall great anime with lovable characters and while there's obviuosly not a lot of action,
it's what I would consider alomst a slice of life anime that's really laid back and fun to watch. The next anime that
I want to discuss as one of my favorite's would probably have to be My Hero Academia. Almost a year ago I started to
watch this anime since I wanted to see what all the buzz was about and I was not disappointed. While I haven't necessarily
finished all of the anime since I've only watched through half of season 4. I have watched most of it and so many aspects of
it are great like the story, the characters, the action and the emotions are all there and make this anime very good in my
opinion. Now, on to my favorite anime which will probably always be my favorite, Jojo's Bizzare Adventure. I don't even know
where to begin with this anime. It's a story that spans over a century where each season just gets better and better and the
chrarcters in each season are very lovable and memorable. The humor is great and the villains in each season are memorable
and great too. I finally watched it earlier this year with my girlfriend since she pushed me to watch it for so long and so
I finally caved in and was not disappointed and surprisingly it became my favorite anime. If you want to know more about each
Jojo, I'll have information on them below this post but otherwise that's it for now. Thank you so much for reading and I hope
you have a good day!
