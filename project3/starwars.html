Hello everyone! In this post I just wanted to share with you my ranking for all of the Star Wars movies
and give some short thoughts on each movie and will only include the episodic films but will provide the two
spinoff movies below where you can create your ranking. So, to begin, let's start at the #1 spot which for me
is EpisodeV: The Empire Strikes Back. This movie is I think the best Star Wars film because of how much it
expands upon Episode IV and the story and the settings in this movie is unbeatable compared to the other films.
At the #2 spot for me is Episode VI: Return of the Jedi. Yes, even with the Ewoks in this movie along with the
slow start at Jabba's Palace this movie is #2 for me since as a kid I watched this one a lot so it has a lot of
nostalgia for me along with how great the end battle between Luke and Vader is combined with the Battle of Endor
makes this movie still very good for me. At the #3 spot I have Episode III: Revenge of the Sith. Even though the
prequels get a bad rep, Episode III is the one that is very good out of the three because of the darker tone and
how well the execution is performed with connecting it to the original trilogy. The lightsaber fight at the end of
the movie is by far one of the best in the series and where the bad guys win for once. At the #4 spot is the movie
that started it all, Episode IV: A New Hope. What's there to say about this movie that hasn't already been said? This
movie is simple, fun, and a classic that was absolutely revolutionary for it's time. It introduced people to the Star
Wars universe for the first time and the story is creative and very good, even if it's simple.

At #5 is the ending of the Skywalker Saga, Episode IX: The Rise of Skywalker. While this movie marked the end of the
Disney trilogy, which now is getting the treatment the prequels were getting when they first released, this movie I
think is the best out of the three because of how much ground the movie covers with the amount of time it's given and
the movie does feel like a love letter to Star Wars fans because of how much fan service is in it. This movie is not at
all perfect and has its problems but it's the most enjoyable one out of the three for me. At #6 is the start of the
Disney trilogy, Episode VII: The Force Awakens. Even though this movie shares a lot of similar elements with A New Hope,
it executes itself as a soft reboot well enough to make this a fun movie that introduced people back into Star Wars and
introduced new characters and ideas. At #7 is what's probably the most hated film now out of the nine, Episode VIII: The
Last Jedi. Yes, this film has some major glaring problems that make this one not as enjoyable for me with things like
Luke's character and the plot itself however, there are some enjoyable things that you can still take away from this
movie such as the throne room fight scene and the Battle of Crait. At the #8 spot is the start of the prequel trilogy,
Episode I: The Phantom Menace. This movie kicked off the prequel trilogy but definitely not with a bang. The only notable
qualities that this movie has is the podracing scene and the lightsaber duel at the end with Darth Maul. Everything else
in this movie feels pointless and that nothing and that nothing really happens. It gives you the feeling that Episode II
really should have been Episode I. And last in my ranking is Episode II: Attack of the Clones. Even with some enjoyable
moments, this movie to me just feels boring since half of it feels like your with Anakin and Padme talking to each other
with not the best dialogue. The things that save the movie for me though is the chase with the bounty hunter near the
beginning of the movie, the fight between Jango and Obi-Wan, the gladiator arena scene with all of the Jedi along with
the Battle of Geonosis, and the lightsaber fights at the end of the movie aren't too shabby either. Well, that's it for
my ranking but feel free to make your own ranking below and thank you for reading this!
