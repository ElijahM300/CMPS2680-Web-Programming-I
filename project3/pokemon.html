Hello Pokemon lovers! Today I wanted to discuss the TCG and some of the cards I've pulled from packs that I've
opened up recently. About a couple of weeks ago I bought more Pokemon cards for the first time in around five
years and at Target I got an Eternatus V tin which containes a promo card and five card packs, each with 10
additional game cards. The very first pack I opened from the tin, which was from the Darkness Ablaze set, I got a
Charizard VMAX card which goes for almost $100! I couldn't believe that the first pack I opened in over five
years I got one of the rarest cards from that set, while for the other four packs I didn't get anything notable.
Then about a week later I went to Gamestop for Black Friday and got four packs since they had a deal of buy one
get one 60% off. All the packs were from Darkness Ablaze and from those packs I was able to get a Butterfree V
Full Art card and a Galarian Slowbro V card, so pretty nice pulls. Then a couple days ago at Target again I got
around eight more packs, six from the newest set, Vivid Voltage, and two from Darkness Abalze. My luck wasn't as
great with these packs since the only notable card I pulled was a Darude V card which is a pretty nice card. I'm
hoping that for Christmas I can get more packs and I hope I have some luck and get nice pulls so wish me luck in
my openings! In other mediums for Pokemon, I recently got back into playing Pokemon Let's Go Pikachu and beat it
the other day after having the game for such a long time. The team I beat the game with was Pikachu, Charizard,
Haunter, Kabutops, Pidgeot, and Vileplume. I beat all of the Elite Four my first time through but lost to the
champion and when I tried again, it was much smoother beating the Elite Four the second time around and I beat the
champion with little problem this time too. My goals now for the game is to try and catch Mewtwo and then try to find a
shiny Charizard, so hopefully I can do both relatively soon. You can open some packs below from Darkness Ablaze and
Vivid Voltage, who knows, you might get something good! All you have to do is click the open button and the cards you
get will show up. That's all for this post so thank you for reading this and see you next time!(As a hint, try double
clicking on the pack itself, not just the button...)
